How to read this

The wall is symbolized by the selected rank field. Watershed boundaries are shown in dark grey for context. Points of interest use colored triangles by type. The viewshed overlay shows where the wall may be visible, and the light layer shows the potential visibility or intensity of light associated with the proposed wall.
